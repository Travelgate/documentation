# 106 Error - Request Aborted by Client

## What Does a 106 Error Mean?
A 106 error is a communication error that occurs when the Buyer (client) closes the connection for a transaction through Travelgate before a response is received from our system.

:::info
Sudden Increase in 106 Errors?
If you observe a sudden or significant spike in 106 errors, please contact our Support Team. Providing our team with the relevant transaction IDs and timestamps will allow us to review the issue.
:::
