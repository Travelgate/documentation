
<details>
  <summary>
  **HotelXFilterSearchInput** (*INPUT_OBJECT*)  
  Filters allow you to precisely tailor the response according to your preferences.  
  </summary>
  
  <details>
    <summary>
      <strong>access</strong> <em>(AccessFilterInput)</em>
    </summary>
    By using the access filter, our system will exclusively include or exclude options from the selected accesses.
    If you opt not to set any access at all, requests will be made to all available accesses.

    <details style={{ pointerEvents: "none" }}>
      <summary>
        <strong>includes</strong> <em>(ID)</em>
      </summary>
      These Access IDs will overwrite the default configuration. Only the IDs on this list will be used in the search query.
    </details>

    <details style={{ pointerEvents: "none" }}>
      <summary>
        <strong>excludes</strong> <em>(ID)</em>
      </summary>
      These Access IDs will overwrite the default configuration. The IDs on this list will be excluded from the search query.
    </details>
  </details>

    <details>
      <summary>
        <strong>includes</strong> <em>(HotelXFilterPluginTypeInput)</em>
      </summary>
      Plugins to be only executed

      <details open>
        <summary>
          <strong>step</strong> <em>(PluginStepType)</em>
        </summary>
        The Step of the plugin to filter. @deprecated(reason: "deprecated from 2025-08-25. This property is no longer recommended for use.")
        <strong>Possible values:</strong>
        <ul>
          <li><code>REQUEST</code>: Plugins executed after the Buyer requests a message to Hotel-X</li>
          <li><code>REQUEST_ACCESS</code>: Plugins executed before sending a request to the supplier using access, and after the Accesses have been calculated</li>
          <li><code>RESPONSE_OPTION</code>: Plugins executed after the supplier responds a message, applied to every option they return</li>
          <li><code>RESPONSE_ACCESS</code>: Plugins executed after all access options have been responded to</li>
          <li><code>RESPONSE</code>: Plugins executed before Hotel-X responds the message to the Buyer</li>
        </ul>
      </details>

    <summary>
      <strong>rateRules</strong> <em>(RateRulesFilterInput)</em>
    </summary>
    This filter enables you to narrow down the options returned by the suppliers based on the desired rate rules you want to include or exclude.

    <details open>
      <summary>
        <strong>includes</strong> <em>(RateRulesType)</em>
      </summary>
      if includes not nil: only options without rate rules and options with rate rules found in includes will be returned
      <strong>Possible values:</strong>
      <ul>
        <li><code>NORMAL</code>: NORMAL Used in the RateRulesFilterInput input for the search query to filter and retrieve only options without rate rules.</li>
        <li><code>PACKAGE</code>: The product can&#39;t be sold separately from another product attached to it, such as a flight.</li>
        <li><code>OLDER55</code>: OLDER55 can only be sold to people who are 55 and older.</li>
        <li><code>OLDER60</code>: OLDER60 can only be sold to people who are 60 and older.</li>
        <li><code>OLDER65</code>: OLDER65 can only be sold to people who are 65 and older.</li>
        <li><code>CANARY_RESIDENT</code>: CANARY_RESIDENT is applicable to Canary Islands residents only.</li>
        <li><code>BALEARIC_RESIDENT</code>: BALEARIC_RESIDENT is applicable to Balearic Islands residents only.</li>
        <li><code>LARGE_FAMILY</code>: LARGE_FAMILY is applicable to large families and is determined by each supplier.</li>
        <li><code>HONEYMOON</code>: HONEYMOON is applicable to those who just got married and is determined by each supplier.</li>
        <li><code>PUBLIC_SERVANT</code>: PUBLIC_SERVANT is applicable to public servants only.</li>
        <li><code>UNEMPLOYED</code>: UNEMPLOYED applies to individuals who are without work.</li>
        <li><code>NON_REFUNDABLE</code>: NON_REFUNDABLE is applicable to non refundable options.</li>
        <li><code>MOBILE</code>: MOBILE are special rates confirmed by mobile phone.</li>
        <li><code>WARRANTY</code>: WARRANTY is applicable to options with cancellation warranty.</li>
        <li><code>ESSENTIAL_WORKER</code>: ESSENTIAL_WORKER are special hotel rates for essential workers.</li>
        <li><code>NEGOTIATED</code>: NEGOTIATED are special rates offered only to specific buyers.</li>
        <li><code>CUSTOM</code>: CUSTOM defines other rates.</li>
      </ul>
    </details>

    <summary>
      <strong>status</strong> <em>(StatusFilterInput)</em>
    </summary>
    Use this filter to determine which status (OK and RQ) will be included or excluded in the response.

    <details open>
      <summary>
        <strong>includes</strong> <em>(StatusType)</em>
      </summary>
      When is not nil: only options with status found in includes will be returned
      <strong>Possible values:</strong>
      <ul>
        <li><code>OK</code>: The status of the avail is available</li>
        <li><code>RQ</code>: The status of the avail is On request</li>
      </ul>
    </details>

    <details open>
      <summary>
        <strong>excludes</strong> <em>(StatusType)</em>
      </summary>
      When is not nil: only options without status found in includes will be returned
      <strong>Possible values:</strong>
      <ul>
        <li><code>OK</code>: The status of the avail is available</li>
        <li><code>RQ</code>: The status of the avail is On request</li>
      </ul>
    </details>
  </details>
