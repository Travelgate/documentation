# Edit Rate

## Edit Existing Hotel Rates

Once your rates are created, you can easily modify and manage them directly at the hotel rate level in the Travelgate Inventory Extranet. Update pricing conditions, cancellation policies, market restrictions, and other rate configurations as needed.

### Steps to Edit a Rate  

1. Navigate to the **Inventory** top menu and select **SetUp**.
2. Select a connection (Client + Provider) and hotel.
3. Click on the relevant rate you want to modify.
4. Update the necessary fields (policies, markets, etc.).
5. Save your changes.

### Best Practices When Editing Rates

**Always coordinate rate changes with your Channel Manager** to ensure synchronization across all distribution channels. Uncoordinated changes may result in pricing discrepancies or booking conflicts.

:::info
For detailed information about rate configuration fields, see our **[Inventory SetUp Documentation](/docs/apps/inventory/extranet/set-up/setup)** guide.
:::
