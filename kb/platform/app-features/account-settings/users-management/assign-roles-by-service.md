# How to Assign Permissions by Service

You can assign different [permissions](/kb/platform/app-features/account-settings/users-management/add-users#permissions-by-service) to users for specific services within your Organization. Here’s how:

1. Click the **three dots** next to the user and select **Modify services permissions**. This feature is only available to **Admins** or **Owners**.
2. Choose the **services** you want the user to access.
3. Assign permissions for each service as needed.
		
	:::info
	- You cannot assign a permission lower than the user’s organization-wide permission.
	- Admin permissions for **Company Management** are required to assign notification permissions. A tooltip will remind you to contact your administrator if you lack these rights.
	:::
